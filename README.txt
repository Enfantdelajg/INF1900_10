My team and I developed a path-following robot that intelligently avoids obstacles. Through collaborative efforts, we programmed its navigation system, integrated sensor data, and implemented obstacle detection algorithms. This project strengthened our teamwork, problem-solving skills, and expertise in robotics, paving the way for exciting future endeavors.
